## Prerequisites

Before importing products to RevenueCat, make sure you have:

- Completed the [RevenueCat setup](./revenuecat)
- Created [Android products](./android-products) in Google Play Console
- Created [iOS products](./ios-products) in App Store Connect
- Tested your purchases with [Android closed testing](./closed-testing) or [iOS sandbox testing](./ios-sandbox)

## Configure Entitlements

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create New Entitlement">
    1. Go to [RevenueCat](https://app.revenuecat.com/)
    2. Select your project
    3. Click **Product catalog** in the left sidebar
    4. Choose **Entitlements** in the top menu
    5. Click **+ New Entitlement**
       - **Identifier:** This identifier will be used in your app to determine level of access (e.g., `premium`)
       - **Description:** A description of what the Entitlement unlocks (e.g., `Premium access to all features`)
    6. Click **Add**
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Import Products from App Stores

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import Products">
    1. Still inside **Product catalog**, click **Products** in the top menu
    2. For each app you want to import products for, click the **Import** button
    3. RevenueCat will display a list of products that are available to import
    4. Choose the products you want to import
    5. Click **Import**
    
    If the import is successful, you will see the products in the list with status **Ready to Submit** or **Published**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Attach Products to Entitlements">
    For each imported product:
    
    1. Click **Attach** in the Entitlements column
    2. **Attach to Entitlement:** Select the entitlement you created in the previous step
    3. Click **Attach**
    
    Repeat this process for all your products.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verification

After completing the import process, verify that:

- All your products appear in the RevenueCat Product catalog
- Each product is attached to the correct entitlement
- Product status shows as **Ready to Submit** or **Published**
- The product identifiers match those in your app stores

## Next Steps

With your products imported and configured in RevenueCat, you can now:

- Set up [Superwall integration](./superwall) for paywall management
- Test your in-app purchases using the configured products
- Implement the subscription logic in your app code
